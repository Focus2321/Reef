# Reef Project Guidance

- Reef is a fork of the original Reef project. Treat its handcrafted foundation and existing architectural choices as intentional unless evidence shows they should change.
- Speed and responsiveness are non-negotiable: shortcuts, the switcher panel, and window cycling must continue to feel instantaneous. Assess performance impact for every change and verify affected interaction paths.
- Prefer small, direct, maintainable solutions that match the existing style. Avoid speculative abstractions, oversized fixes, and short-term workarounds that create long-term technical debt.
- Keep PRs short, focused, and internally consistent. Do not bundle unrelated refactors with fixes; undertake large refactors only when explicitly requested or clearly necessary.
